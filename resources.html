Technical Q&A Answered During Evaluation

Q: Can call recordings (not just Zoom) use AI assist functionality?

‚ñº

A: Absolutely. The same functionality applies‚Äîyou get the same page, all search capabilities, playlist capabilities, and assist functionality on calls.

Q: How quickly are AI insights available?

‚ñº

A: Calls are recorded live, so AI insights are available almost instantaneously after the call ends.

Q: Does the assist button pull from the right information?

‚ñº

A: The assist button scans the last 80 emails and 10 recorded calls. If pricing or specific topics come up in any of those engagements, it will surface in the assist results.

Q: Can you search for specific keywords across opportunities?

‚ñº

A: Yes, you can search for specific keywords (like "pricing") and it will search through all calls and pop open specific timestamps where those keywords were mentioned.

Q: Do calls, emails, and tasks push into Salesforce as activities?

‚ñº

A: Yes, every call, email, and meeting booked pushes into Salesforce records.

Q: Are there third-party texting integrations that can be automated in sequences?

‚ñº

A: Unfortunately there isn't a great third-party texting provider for full automation due to legal restrictions and compliance requirements. Outreach has its own texting solution with some limitations.

Watch: Recorded Demo of Outreach's Texting Solution (~9 mins)

Q: Can you report on meeting types booked through Chili Piper?

‚ñº

A: If using Chili Piper exclusively for booking, meeting types won't natively filter in Outreach reports. The workaround is building meeting type reports in Salesforce (Chili Piper pushes meeting type to SF), but you can't filter by CP meeting types in Outreach reports directly.

Q: How does historical Salesforce reporting work?

‚ñº

A: Advanced task mapping will push individual calls, emails, and tasks as separate entities to Salesforce, allowing you to maintain historical comp reporting while transitioning to Outreach.

Q: Is there a heat map for calls by days in the cadence?

‚ñº

A: Yes, there's a heat map by time of day and day of week. You can filter down by team to get granular around when they're specifically getting answers.

Q: Can you customize dashboards?

‚ñº

A: Yes, you can create custom dashboards where you drag and move boxes around like apps on an iPhone. All dashboards can be set as default and are fully customizable.

Q: How does topic tracking work?

‚ñº

A: You can filter recordings by specific topics (like "Prime product") and call dispositions to see how you're booking meetings by leveraging specific products or messaging.

Q: Can the AI assist search for competitor mentions?

‚ñº

A: Yes, you can search for competitor names across all calls and create playlists for best-in-class examples of handling competitive situations.

Q: Does Chili Piper integrate with Outreach?

‚ñº

A: Yes, lots of customers use Chili Piper with Outreach. Teams can use the Chili Piper extension in Salesforce while working with Outreach's Salesforce extension.

Q: Can leads vs contacts data be aggregated?

‚ñº

A: Yes, because data is collected in Outreach with bidirectional sync, you can get collective reporting across leads and contacts, avoiding the Salesforce limitation of choosing leads OR contacts reports.
